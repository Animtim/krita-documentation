Useful Regexp when converting:

----------
menuchoice
----------


<span class="menuchoice">([\w\s]+)<\/span>

to

:guilabel:`\1`

<span class="menuchoice">([\w\s]+)<\/span>

to

:menuselection:`\1`

(When using menu selections, replace &rarr; and -> to look like -->)

---
kbd
---
<kbd>([\w\s->]+)<\/kbd>

to

:kbd:`\1`


--------------------------------
inline references(internal links
--------------------------------

\[\[([\w\s->]+)\|[\w\s->]+\]\] 

to

:ref:`\1`
